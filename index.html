About Us

Mod's Thai Kitchen is a women-owned, small business based in Evanston, IL. Our founder, Mod Boon-Long, is a Thai native with lots of experience cooking Thai food when she wants a little taste of home!

When Mod noticed how difficult it was to find authentic Thai ingredients, she saw an opportunity to start Mod's Thai Kitchen to make it easier than ever to find and shop for Thai products.

We are so excited to help you create a delicious Thai meal right from the comfort of your home!
